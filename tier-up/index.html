Greeting/Order of the Day (OOD)

Steps to the Sale - After the "Yes"

1
Complete Credit
Submitted on iPad — OWNER with Rep Guidance
Paper credit application needed if:
  • Anyone is not running credit (both or secondary)
  • An international buyer
  • Cashout
  • WAAM
2
Collect Down Payment and Autopay
Turn in with your deal/reference number — REP with Owner
3
Final Pitch Created
T.O./SM
  • Verify 30-45 days for first-payment date
  • DOB, SSN, address, phone and email are added and correct
  • All commissions in place
  • All incentives are in place (bonus credits/points, OTI)
  • Correct inventory is selected
  • Down payment has been double-checked
4
WSA Info Reviewed with Owner
REP
  • Names, address, phone, email
  • Terms of purchase
    • Down payment amount and type
    • Dues and loan amount and type
    • Amount financed
If any information is not correct
1. Get corrected info and go back to step 3!
5
PAT Completed
REP with Owner
  • Process down payment through Navigator
    • New WRV card does not require card reader
    • Personal CC needs card reader
  • Autopay – Get a new card for dues/loan payments
6
Submit Deal into Teams
REP or T.O. or Anyone if information is shared clearly
  • Verify owner has access to email being entered
  • Enter email for each owner if they want copies of the DS to separate email addresses
  • Notes that outline any one-off scenario along with DBM (Dominant Buying Motive)
7
Book Confirmation
View books in email from DocuSign — OWNER with Rep Guidance
  • Have owner open email and click Review to open the DocuSign
  • Follow Teams chat on when it has been sent
8
ORF Review
Review with owner line by line — REP with Owner (owner should not be reviewing by themselves)
  • Review line by line
  • If anything is missing/mistyped notify your OO via Teams chat immediately
  • NOA – if your OO is onsite, bring them to your table for soft intro
9
Submit Service Promise Form (SPF)
AFTER ORF REVIEW — REP with Owner
10
Zoom is Ready
Move owners to OO closing room and make sure all technology is set up — REP
  • Sit in closing while OO is going through presentation (be present just like an SP)
  • You have turned the deal over to the OO team. If assistance is needed, OO will ask
11
Gifting
Bring owner to OPS for gifting — REP with Owner
12
Follow Up
Keep calls/texts simple, not about the purchase — REP A/REP B
  • Within 24 hours
  • Within 48-72 hours
  • On day 7
Examples of follow ups:
"Thank you for your business with us" • "I hope you are having/had a great vacation" • "What did you have for lunch/dinner" • "Ask them about the sites or activities you recommended" • "Did you make it home safely"
